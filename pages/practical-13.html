About

Full stack development refers to the development of both front end(client side) and back end(server side) portions of web application. Full stack web developers have the ability to design complete web application and websites. They work on the frontend, backend, database and debugging of web application or websites. They are proficient in both front-end and back-end languages and frameworks, as well as in server, network and hosting environments. To get to this breadth and depth of knowledge, most full-stack developers will have spent many years working in a variety of different roles. They also tend to be well-versed in both business logic and user experience, meaning they are not only well-equipped to get hands on, but can also guide and consult on strategy too.
